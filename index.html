About India's Heritage

India's historical heritage is a rich tapestry of art, architecture, religion, and culture dating back thousands of years. From ancient temples and majestic forts to UNESCO World Heritage Sites, India reflects a deep-rooted legacy that continues to inspire the world.

Famous Heritage Sites

  • Taj Mahal – A symbol of eternal love in Agra.
  • Qutub Minar – A soaring tower in Delhi from the Delhi Sultanate era.
  • Hampi – The ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire in Karnataka.
  • Khajuraho Temples – Known for intricate carvings and sculpture.
  • Ajanta & Ellora Caves – Rock-cut Buddhist and Hindu cave temples in Maharashtra.
